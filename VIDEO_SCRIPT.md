# Demo Video Script: SceneSound Studio Pro

Target length: 2-4 minutes.

## 0:00-0:25 - Opening

"This is SceneSound Studio Pro, my CS 5588 Week 15 final project. It builds directly on my Week 13 animal-care image generation project and my Week 14 SceneSound audio prototype. For Week 15, I combined those ideas into a polished multimodal AI studio for educational animal-care media."

## 0:25-1:10 - Project Progression

"In Week 13, the project focused on structured prompts for Stable Diffusion-style animal health visualization. In Week 14, SceneSound Studio focused on music beds and sound effects from text prompts. The final version keeps the same creative foundation-model direction but adds a complete workflow: image prompt, soundtrack prompt, SFX cue sheet, narration, safety review, and evaluation."

## 1:10-2:05 - Workflow Demo

Open the Gradio app.

"The user enters an animal, care scenario, environment, soundtrack mood, duration, and target audience. For example, I can create a golden retriever scene about mild dehydration warning signs in a veterinary clinic for first-time pet owners."

Click "Generate Multimodal Plan."

## 2:05-2:55 - Outputs

Show each tab:

- Image Prompt
- Music Prompt
- SFX Cue Sheet
- Narration
- Responsible AI
- Evaluation Metrics

"The final output is not just a single prompt. It is a production brief. The image prompt supports the Week 13 visual direction, the music prompt and SFX cues build on Week 14, and the narration plus safety review make it more presentation-ready and responsible."

## 2:55-3:30 - Evaluation and Business Value

Open `outputs/evaluation_results.csv`.

"For evaluation, I compare a Week 14 audio-only baseline with the Week 15 multimodal system. I measure prompt alignment, multimodal quality, realism, diversity, latency, safety flags, and SFX cue coverage. The target users are veterinary educators, animal shelters, pet-care creators, instructors, and small marketing teams."

## 3:30-4:00 - Close

"SceneSound Studio Pro is significantly better than Week 14 because it turns an audio prototype into a complete multimodal AI product. It has stronger engineering, better outputs, deeper evaluation, improved user experience, clearer business value, and responsible-AI safeguards."
